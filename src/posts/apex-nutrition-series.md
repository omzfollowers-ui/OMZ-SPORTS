*Presented by Apex Nutrition. This series is created by OMZ Brand Studio in collaboration with our partner and is distinct from independent newsroom reporting.*

Ask a professional athlete about nutrition and you rarely get a story about willpower. You get a story about logistics — about the chef who travels with the squad, the recovery shake timed to the final whistle, the unglamorous discipline of eating well on a Tuesday in February when nobody is watching.

The OMZ x Apex Nutrition Performance Series sets out to tell those stories properly. Over the coming weeks we will sit down with athletes across football, basketball, and endurance sport to understand how fuelling decisions are made across a long season, and how the science has shifted away from rigid rules toward something more individual.

The first instalment follows a top-flight midfielder through a match-week, from the carbohydrate loading that begins three days out to the protein-and-sleep protocol that governs the 48 hours after. The details are specific, occasionally surprising, and a useful corrective to the supplement-aisle mythology that surrounds the subject.

For Apex Nutrition, the series is a chance to show the thinking behind their products rather than simply advertise them. For readers, it is a window into a part of elite sport that usually stays behind closed doors. New episodes publish every Thursday.
